About this app

PR Urban Brain is your business operating manual: every SOP, checklist, contact and playbook in one searchable place. Tap any article to read it, hit Edit to change it, and the + button to add a new one. Changes sync instantly to everyone connected. Articles containing [FILL IN] still need your real details.

How this app works, in one breath: the Brain is the business operating manual — every SOP, checklist, contact and playbook in one searchable place, shared with the whole team. If it's not written in the Brain, it only exists in someone's head. Read it, edit it, add to it — it syncs to everyone instantly.
Finding things — search & sections
What: the search bar looks through every word of every article — titles and content. The coloured chips filter by section: SOPs (how we do things, step by step), Staff (people, onboarding, conduct, logins), Vault (key info, contacts, compliance), Marketing (growing the business).

How: type any word — "refund", "blue card", "closing" — and matching articles appear instantly. Tap a chip to see one section only; tap All to reset.

When: before asking Paiige anything — the answer is probably in here. And before writing a new article, search first so you don't duplicate one.
Reading an article
How: tap any card to open it. Headings, numbered steps and bullets are formatted for easy skimming. Yellow [FILL IN] tags mark details that still need real information — if you know the answer, hit Edit and fill it in!

The footer shows which section it belongs to and when it was last edited — a very old date on a fast-changing topic (prices, contacts) is a hint to double-check it's still true.
Editing & adding articles
Edit: open an article → Edit → change → Save. Everyone sees the update at once.

Add: the pink + button (bottom-right of the Articles list) → title, section, content → Save.

Delete: inside Edit — tap Delete twice (the first tap asks "sure?"). Deleting is forever, so prefer editing an article to killing it.

Why it matters: the golden rule of the Brain — when you figure something out, write it down; when something changes, update it. A stale manual is worse than none.
What lives where — the four sections
SOPs — opening/closing, running classes, enrolments, payments, cancellations, complaints, incidents, trials. The "how we do things" of the studio.

Staff — onboarding checklist, code of conduct, getting logins, working-with-children rules (Blue Cards!), payroll.

Vault — where everything lives, key contacts & suppliers, term dates & pricing, insurance/licensing/compliance dates.

Marketing — brand voice, weekly posting routine, content ideas bank, term-launch campaign playbook, DM answers. (The day-to-day posting tracker is the separate PR Urban Social app.)
Sync, sharing with staff & backups
Sharing: this app is meant to be shared with staff — send them the link and the Brain sync code, they paste it once, done. The code works like a key: anyone holding it can read and edit.

Never paste the household finance or calendar codes into any article — those are private to Paiige & Micky.

If a staff member leaves on bad terms, Paiige can move the Brain to a fresh code (ask Claude — takes minutes) and share the new one with remaining staff.

Backup: Sync tab → Download Backup, monthly-ish and after big writing sessions. Keep the file with the other app backups.
